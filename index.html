Who are we? What we do?

ASAP AI utilizes artificial intelligent technology to develop, maintain and optimize communities around us

We aim to provide solutions to advance communities that could benefit from modelling and prediction technology. Projects will encompass identifying a system that needs implementing/updating, and designing an appropriate solution.

Utilizing supervised, unsupervised, semi-supervised and reinforcement learning concepts, we will be able to improve the efficiency and well-being of the systems our country operates under.
